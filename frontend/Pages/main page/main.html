About Us

Our journey towards a healthier, stronger you.

  • 2019-2020

    Starting with Passion

    We began as a small team of fitness enthusiasts dedicated to tracking our own progress and sharing what we learned. This passion for improvement became the foundation of our gym tracker platform.

  • March 2021

    A Community is Born

    With our early success, we decided to open the platform to everyone, welcoming people of all fitness levels to track their journey, connect with others, and set ambitious goals.

  • December 2022

    Expanding Our Features

    We listened to our users and introduced advanced tracking, personalized workout plans, and progress metrics to help everyone stay motivated and on track to reach their fitness goals.

  • July 2023

    Building for the Future

    As we grow, so does our commitment to innovation in fitness tracking. We’re continuously working to bring new tools, integrations, and community features to enhance your experience.
